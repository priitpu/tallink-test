# Grid system

The grid system uses a series of containers, rows, and columns to layout and align content.

The base of a Grid is `<Grid.Base>`, followed by a `<Grid.Row>` filled with `<Grid.Column>`.

### Columns
The grid is split into 4 columns on screens smaller than 600px, 6 columns on screens smaller than 1024px and 12 columns on anything wider than that.
